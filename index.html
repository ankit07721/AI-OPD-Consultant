AI-Based OPD Consultant for Paediatric Care

Intelligent Clinical Decision Support System for Pediatric Outpatient Consultations

Step 1: Clinical Problem Definition

Define what the AI consultant will predict and support

🔍 Disease Prediction

Predict probable pediatric diseases based on patient symptoms and medical history

📊 Severity Classification

Classify disease severity into categories: Mild / Moderate / Severe

💡 Investigation Recommendations

Suggest appropriate investigations: X-ray, MRI, Rest, Blood tests, etc.

🎯 Clinical Decision Support

Provide evidence-based recommendations aligned with clinical guidelines

Step 2: Data Collection & Preprocessing

Sample Input Features

  • Demographics: Age, Gender
  • Symptomatology: Pain location, Pain duration
  • Physical Findings: Swelling (Yes/No), Range of motion
  • History: Trauma history (Yes/No)
  • Assessment: Pain severity score (0-10)
  • Vital Signs: Temperature, Heart rate, Blood pressure

Target Output (Labels)

  • Diagnosis Class: Primary diagnostic prediction
  • Severity Level: Mild / Moderate / Severe
  • Recommended Tests: Investigation list
  • Clinical Notes: Additional observations
